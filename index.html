Crystals Collector

Welcome to the Crystals Collector Game! The number you see below is your Target Number.

The four crystals below the Target Number each contain their own randomized value. You do not know what their value is until you click on them.

To win, you need to match the total of the crystals to the Target Number. You will lose if you go over the Target Number.

When you lose, the game will restart with a new Target Number and new values for each crystal.
